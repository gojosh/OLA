Christmastide

The Feast of the Holy Family

Sunday within the Octave of Christmas
December XXVIII

Today the Church celebrates the Holy Family of Jesus, Mary, and Joseph— a model of domestic sanctity for all Christian households, established as a universal feast in 1921 by Pope Benedict XV.

From The Liturgical Year

This Sunday has been chosen by the Church for the celebration of the Feast of the Holy Family; the liturgy of the day harmonizes well with the mystery of the new Feast, for it carries us forward to the childhood of our Emmanuel and gives us those wonderful words: "And he went down with them and came to Nazareth and was subject to them."

The Feast was welcomed by succeeding Pontiffs as an efficacious means for bringing home to the Christian people the example of the Holy Family at Nazareth.

Dom Prosper Guéranger, O.S.B. — Abbot of Solesmes
